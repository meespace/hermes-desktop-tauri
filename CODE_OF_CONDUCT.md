# Code of Conduct / 行为准则

## 我们希望这里是什么样 / The Kind of Space We Want

中文：

这个仓库欢迎直接、专业、讲道理的讨论，也欢迎不同意见。我们不要求每个人说话都很圆滑，但希望每个人都能把注意力放在问题本身，而不是把火力对准对方。

English:

This repository welcomes direct, practical, and thoughtful discussion, including disagreement. Nobody needs to sound polished all the time, but everyone is expected to keep the focus on the problem rather than turning the conversation against the person.

## 我们鼓励的行为 / Expected Behavior

中文：

- 对事不对人
- 提意见时尽量具体，别只丢情绪
- 假设别人是想把事情做好，再判断分歧在哪里
- 给新贡献者一点耐心
- 引用事实、日志、截图、代码，而不是靠气势压人
- 如果你错了，愿意改；如果别人改对了，愿意承认

English:

- Argue the point, not the person
- Be specific when giving feedback instead of dropping pure frustration
- Start from the assumption that the other person is trying to help, then work out the disagreement
- Give newer contributors some patience
- Use facts, logs, screenshots, and code instead of trying to win by force of tone
- Be willing to correct yourself, and willing to acknowledge when someone else got it right

## 我们不能接受的行为 / Unacceptable Behavior

中文：

下面这些行为在这里不欢迎：

- 人身攻击、羞辱、嘲讽、威胁
- 歧视性言论
- 持续骚扰
- 故意挑衅、钓鱼、恶意带节奏
- 泄露他人隐私信息
- 明知会让协作失控，还反复推动冲突升级

English:

These behaviors are not welcome here:

- personal attacks, humiliation, mockery, or threats
- discriminatory language
- repeated harassment
- deliberate baiting, trolling, or bad-faith escalation
- exposing someone else's private information
- repeatedly pushing a discussion into conflict after it is already clearly getting out of hand

## 维护者会怎么处理 / How Maintainers May Respond

中文：

维护者可以根据情况采取这些措施：

- 提醒
- 要求回到问题本身
- 删除不合适的评论
- 锁定讨论
- 拒绝或关闭相关 issue / PR
- 在必要时限制或移除参与权限

English:

Maintainers may respond with any of the following, depending on the situation:

- a warning
- a request to return to the actual issue
- removal of inappropriate comments
- locking a discussion
- closing or rejecting the related issue or pull request
- restricting or removing participation when necessary

## 如果你遇到问题 / If Something Goes Wrong

中文：

如果你遇到的是行为问题，而不是普通技术分歧，请尽量不要在公开讨论里继续拉扯。可以先在仓库里发一个简短说明，请维护者接手处理；如果内容涉及个人信息或敏感细节，请不要公开贴出来。

English:

If the problem is about conduct rather than an ordinary technical disagreement, please try not to keep dragging it out in a public thread. Open a short note in the repository asking a maintainer to step in; if private or sensitive details are involved, do not post them publicly.

## 最后一句 / One Last Thing

中文：

好协作不等于没有分歧。真正重要的是，分歧出现之后，大家还能不能把事情往前推进。

English:

Good collaboration does not mean the absence of disagreement. What matters is whether we can keep the work moving once disagreement shows up.
